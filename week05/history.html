History

"The word azure is derived from various different languages for blue.

French: azur | Italian: azzurro | Polish: lazur | Romanian: azur and azuriu | Portuguese and Spanish: azul | Hungarian azur.

Azure also comes from the mineral azurite. .

Azure is also seen as a symbolic color of calmness and serenity.

In psychology, azure is seen as a refreshing and relaxing color.
